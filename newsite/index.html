Why Crunch? Speed.

Powerful analytics with no programming.

  • Cross-tabs and graphs in 2 clicks.
  • Check awareness among female millennial Californians compared to last year... in 1 minute.
  • Create and export a complete set of tabs in 2 minutes.
  • No more 2-day turnarounds with experts to answer simple questions.

More speed

Real-time surveys

Expectations are rising for shorter, more frequent surveys. Older tools just weren’t built for that. Crunch is.

Stream your data into Crunch in real-time and answer “How is the ad campaign I started yesterday doing?”

Cut housekeeping time substantially. Simple drag and drop to:

  • Combine categories
  • Organize and relabel variables
  • Estimate weights

Scripts automate everything. “Look Ma, No hands!”

Even more speed

Compare current results to the past, instantly.

  • Crunch was created for survey data and so it naturally understands and helps you with tracking studies.
  • All your survey data: past and present, from current and past vendors, plays together nicely inside the CrunchDB.
  • Append datasets together -- Add another “wave” of a tracking study.
  • Join datasets together that share some useful information e.g. demographic information from another survey.

No more digging around to find past data files.
