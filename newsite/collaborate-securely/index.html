Collaborate securely

Crunch allows everyone in your organization to work on the same dataset without having to keep track of individual files.

Collaborate better.

You can control which parts of the dataset the users can access, and group users into teams to make sure that everyone can access the data they need.

Keep up to date.

Crunch datasets can be shared with clients to ensure that they are always looking at the most accurate, up to date information.

Data from Crunch can be shared with with the public using CrunchBoxes, a special purpose widget that allows you to embed Crunch data in any website.
