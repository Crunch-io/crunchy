Our origins

In 2012, a group of survey experts, data scientists, and engineers were frustrated with needing to use many disconnected tools to compile, analyze, and deliver survey data. We set out to make Crunch: a single tool that would allow researchers, analysts, and clients to collaborate in a secure, cloud-based environment.

Our mission

Facilitate survey analysis at scale

Modern survey analysis requires users to efficiently process huge volumes of data, and quickly share their results with their clients. Our mission is to create a platform which allows users to elegantly process any survey dataset, and quickly communicate their results to clients.

Join our team. Help us build the survey analysis tool of the future.
